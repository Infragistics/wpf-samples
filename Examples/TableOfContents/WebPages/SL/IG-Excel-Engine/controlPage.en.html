Import and export Microsoft® Excel® workbooks in high-fidelity with the compiled assembly that reads and writes in the widely-interoperable XLS/XLSX file formats. With absolutely no Excel dependency, your Silverlight application can run completely independent of Excel, and Excel does not have to be installed alongside your application.
